Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What's the minimum stay?

Minimum stay is typically 2-3 nights. For peak periods (Christmas, New Year, Bank Holidays) longer minimum stays may apply.

How do I book?

All bookings start with an enquiry. We'll check availability, confirm pricing, and then take a deposit to secure your dates. Balance is due 8 weeks before arrival.

Can we bring pets?

Yes! We welcome up to 2 well-behaved dogs. Please let us know when booking.

Is Hope Mansell Hall suitable for hen parties?

Hen parties are welcome. With the private bar, games room, and space for 40, it's perfect for celebrations. Please let us know if you're booking for a hen/stag when you enquire.

Can we arrange catering?

Yes, professional catering can be arranged so you don't have to cook at all. We can also arrange wellness treatments and other extras.

What's the parking situation?

We have off-road parking for 20 vehicles. More than enough for even the largest groups.
