Not Just Another Holiday Let

Hope Mansell Hall is a Georgian manor house nestled in a private wooded valley in Herefordshire, offering the kind of space, privacy, and grandeur that most large groups can only dream of.

With 11 acres of gardens and woodland, your own private lake, and rooms designed for bringing people together, this is the perfect setting for celebrations that bring 40 people together as one.
