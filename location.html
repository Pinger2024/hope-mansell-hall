Herefordshire

The Wye Valley and beyond on your doorstep

Perfect Location

In the Heart of the Wye Valley

Hope Mansell Hall sits in a private wooded valley near Ross-on-Wye, where the borders of Herefordshire, Gloucestershire and Monmouthshire meet. This is one of England's most beautiful and unspoilt corners.

Despite the seclusion, you're within easy reach of market towns, riverside walks, and world-class attractions. The perfect balance of escape and exploration.

Things to Do

Attractions Nearby

The River Wye

10 minute drive All ages

One of Britain's most scenic rivers, perfect for kayaking, canoeing, or leisurely boat trips. The Wye Valley is an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, with spectacular views at every turn.

Ross-on-Wye

5 minute drive All ages

A charming market town with independent shops, cafes, and restaurants. The famous Man of Ross celebrated its philanthropy, and today it remains a lovely place to explore on foot.

Symonds Yat

15 minute drive Active groups

Dramatic viewpoints over the river gorge, with peregrine falcons often spotted soaring below. Riverside pubs, canoe hire, and walking trails make this a must-visit for any group.

Forest of Dean

15 minute drive Easy walking

Ancient royal hunting forest with miles of trails for walking and cycling. Visit Puzzlewood (famous as a Star Wars filming location), go ape through the treetops, or simply enjoy the woodland atmosphere.

Herefordshire Vineyards

20 minute drive Adults

English wine is having its moment, and Herefordshire has some excellent vineyards offering tours and tastings. Perfect for a hen party or friends reunion - book ahead for group visits.
